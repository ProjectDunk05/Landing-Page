Brighton & Hove Albion Football Club

Brighton & Hove Albion Football Club, commonly referred to simply as Brighton, is an English professional football club based in the city of Brighton and Hove. They compete in the Premier League. The club's home ground is the 31,800-capacity Falmer Stadium also known as “The AMEX”. The club was founded in 1901 and they are nicknamed the “Seagulls”.
